REMEDIES

Stress

  • Regardless of your current thoughts, ignoring everything around you, set and pursue regular physical activities
  • Simply practice the relaxation techniques such as deep breathing, yoga, massage
  • Develop and keep a sense of humor. Learn to laugh at yourself.
  • Regardless of your current feelings, spend time with your friends and family regularly.
  • Take a regular interest in your hobbies such as reading books, listening to music, and sports, and take out time for them.

Depression

  • of your current thoughts, ignoring everything around you, set and pursue regular physical activities
  • Avoiding alcohol and drugs.
  • Try Stress management techniques
  • Always break big tasks down into smaller tasks and then pursue them.
  • Try to spend time with your loved ones to know your own worth.
  • Get plenty of sleep: Lack of sleep can worsen symptoms of depression. Aim for 7-9 hours of sleep each night.

Anxiety

  • Understand what makes you anxious and avoid it.
  • Herbal remedies: Some herbs, such as valerian root and chamomile, are known for their calming effects and can be taken as teas or supplements.
  • A healthy diet: Eating a balanced and nutritious diet can help improve your overall well-being and may reduce anxiety symptoms.
  • Spending time in nature: Spending time in nature has been shown to have a calming effect and can help reduce anxiety.
  • Limiting caffeine and alcohol: Caffeine and alcohol are stimulants and can worsen anxiety symptoms. Limiting or avoiding them may be helpful.
